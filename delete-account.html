Delete Your Account

Last updated: July 2026

We're sorry to see you go. This page explains how to permanently delete your Locvia account and what happens to your data.

01

Delete from within the App (Recommended)

  • Open the Locvia App
  • Go to Profile → Settings → Delete Account
  • Confirm the deletion when prompted
  • Your account is permanently deleted immediately
02

Request Deletion via Email

No longer have App access? Request deletion via our Contact page. Include the phone number registered with Locvia so we can locate your account. For your security, we will never ask for a password or OTP by email — Locvia doesn't use passwords at all.

Processing time: Email deletion requests are processed within 7 business days.

03

What Gets Deleted

  • Your profile (name, phone number, location details)
  • Your active and past listings, including photos
  • Your chat conversations and messages
  • Your saved/favourited listings
  • Your authentication credentials
04

What May Be Retained

We may retain limited data briefly where required for legal, security, or fraud-prevention purposes (e.g. Terms violations), even post-deletion — never used for any other purpose, and removed once the retention period ends.

05

Need Help?

Facing an issue deleting your account, or have questions about your data? Reach us via Contact and we'll assist promptly.
