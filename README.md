# Locvia — India's Neighbourhood Operating System

Official website and legal pages for **Locvia**, a hyperlocal marketplace connecting buyers and sellers within their neighbourhood.

🔗 **Live site:** [locviahq.github.io/locvia-web](https://locviahq.github.io/locvia-web/)

## About

Locvia lets you buy and sell with people actually near you — no shipping, no waiting, no strangers from across the country. Just your neighbourhood, digitized.

**Buy. Sell. Near You.**
