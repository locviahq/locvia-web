Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 2026

Locvia ("we", "us", "our") operates the Locvia mobile application (the "App"), a hyperlocal marketplace connecting buyers and sellers within their neighbourhood. This policy explains what we collect, how we use it, and the choices you have.

01

Information We Collect

Phone Number. Used to verify your identity via OTP authentication. Required to create an account, never shared directly with other users.

Profile Information. Your name, state, district, area, and mohalla — provided by you during signup — to personalize your marketplace experience.

Location Data. With your permission, we access your device location to show listings within your 6km radius. You can decline and enter your area manually instead.

Listings & Content. Title, description, price, category, and photos for any listing you create.

Photos. Profile and listing images, stored securely via Firebase Cloud Storage.

Chat Messages. Stored to enable in-app conversation history.

Usage & Device Data. Device type, OS, app version, and crash logs to improve stability.

02

How We Use Your Information

  • To create and manage your account
  • To show you listings and sellers near your location
  • To enable buyer–seller chat
  • To send push notifications via Firebase Cloud Messaging
  • To detect and prevent fraud, spam, or abuse
  • To improve app performance and fix bugs
03

Data Storage & Security

Your data is stored using Google Firebase (Firestore, Cloud Storage, Authentication), encrypted in transit (HTTPS/TLS) and at rest. OTP codes are never stored after verification. We use reasonable technical and organizational safeguards, though no internet transmission is 100% secure.

04

Data Sharing

We do not sell your personal data. Your name, area, and listing details are visible to other users as part of core marketplace functionality. We share data with service providers (Firebase/Google Cloud) only to operate the App, and with law enforcement only if legally required.

05

Your Rights & Choices

  • Edit or delete your listings anytime from within the App
  • Update your profile from Settings
  • Disable location access from device settings and enter your area manually
  • Request full account and data deletion — see Delete Account

Data Protection (India): Processed in line with the Digital Personal Data Protection Act, 2023 — we collect only what's necessary and retain it only as long as needed.

06

Children's Privacy

Locvia is not intended for users under 18. We do not knowingly collect data from minors — contact us if you believe a minor holds an account and we'll remove it.

07

Changes & Contact

We may update this policy — changes appear here with a new date. Continued use after changes means acceptance. Questions? Reach us via Contact.
