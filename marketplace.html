Legal

Marketplace Policy

Last updated: July 2026

This Marketplace Policy governs how buyers and sellers use Locvia to list, discover, and transact locally, and sets out the items, services, and conduct that are prohibited on the Service. It is incorporated by reference into our Terms & Conditions. Locvia is a technology platform that connects nearby users; we are not a party to any transaction and do not guarantee the outcome of any listing.

01

Part A — Seller Policy

A.1 Listing Accuracy

Sellers must post listings that accurately describe the item or service, its condition, price, and any material defects. Photographs must genuinely represent the item being sold; using stock photos or images of a different item to misrepresent condition or authenticity is prohibited and may be treated as fraud under Part D.

A.2 Pricing

Sellers set their own prices. Locvia does not verify, endorse, or guarantee that any listed price reflects fair market value. Bait-and-switch pricing — advertising an item at one price with no intention of selling at that price — is prohibited.

A.3 Availability and Fulfillment

Sellers should mark a listing as sold or remove it promptly once an item is no longer available, to avoid misleading buyers. Repeated failure to honor agreed transactions, or failure to disclose known issues, may result in reports and enforcement action under our Report & Enforcement Policy.

A.4 Category-Specific Standards

For "Services" listings, sellers must accurately represent their qualifications and must not misrepresent licensure, certification, or experience where such claims are made. As Room/Rent, Jobs, and other categories expand, additional category-specific standards will be published and incorporated into this Policy.

02

Part B — Buyer Policy

B.1 Due Diligence

Buyers are solely responsible for inspecting items, verifying seller claims, and satisfying themselves as to the suitability, safety, and legality of any purchase before completing a transaction. Locvia does not inspect, certify, or guarantee any listed item or service.

B.2 Respectful Conduct

Buyers must not use in-app chat to harass sellers, make bad-faith offers intended to waste a seller's time, or repeatedly cancel agreed meetings without reasonable notice. Abusive conduct toward sellers is subject to reporting and enforcement under our Report & Enforcement Policy.

B.3 Payment Responsibility

Locvia does not currently process or guarantee payments between users. Buyers who choose to make advance payments to a seller before receiving or inspecting an item do so entirely at their own risk. See Part D for guidance on recognizing high-risk payment requests.

03

Part C — Prohibited Items and Services

The following items, services, and content may not be listed, offered, sold, or exchanged on Locvia under any circumstances. This list is illustrative and not exhaustive; Locvia may remove any listing that we determine, in our sole discretion, to be unsafe, unlawful, or inappropriate for the platform.

  • Weapons, firearms, ammunition, explosives, or components designed to convert an item into a weapon.
  • Illegal drugs, narcotics, or controlled substances, and any paraphernalia primarily intended for illegal drug use.
  • Alcohol and tobacco products, where prohibited by applicable state or national law, or where the seller cannot verify the buyer's legal age.
  • Counterfeit or unauthorized replica goods bearing another party's trademark or branding.
  • Stolen property, or property for which the seller cannot demonstrate lawful ownership.
  • Live animals sold in violation of applicable wildlife protection, animal welfare, or import/export law; endangered species and their parts under any circumstance.
  • Human organs, blood, bodily fluids, or any content related to organ trafficking.
  • Government-issued identification, currency, postage, or documents, real or replica.
  • Recalled products, hazardous chemicals, and items posing a serious risk of injury.
  • Sexually explicit content or services, including any content involving minors (see our Community Guidelines for our zero-tolerance CSAM policy).
  • Any item or service that is illegal to sell, own, or use under the law of India or the buyer's or seller's jurisdiction.
04

Part D — Fraud and Scam Prevention

D.1 Common Scam Patterns

Be cautious of listings or messages that: request payment before any in-person meeting or inspection; pressure you to act urgently or bypass normal caution; offer prices significantly below market value with vague justification; ask you to communicate or pay outside the app immediately after first contact; or request sensitive personal, banking, or OTP information under any pretext.

Locvia will never ask you to share your OTP with another user — OTPs are used solely for account login.

D.2 Reporting Fraud

If you suspect a listing or user is engaged in fraud, use the in-app Report feature immediately and avoid further engagement with the suspected account. Locvia may suspend accounts exhibiting patterns consistent with fraud, including rapid account creation, repeated reports from multiple independent users, or known scam indicators, pending investigation.

D.3 Money Laundering and Suspicious Activity

Locvia prohibits the use of the Service to facilitate money laundering or the transfer of illicit proceeds. We reserve the right to report suspicious activity to relevant authorities, including under the Prevention of Money Laundering Act, 2002 (India), where applicable to our operations.

05

Part E — Marketplace Safety

E.1 Meeting in Person

When meeting another user to complete a transaction, we strongly recommend: meeting in a well-lit, public place such as a market, mall, or police station "safe exchange" zone where available; bringing a friend or family member; meeting during daylight hours where possible; and never sharing your home address before establishing trust with the other party.

E.2 Personal Information

Never share your OTP, bank account details, card numbers, passwords, or government ID numbers with another user through Locvia chat. Locvia's in-app chat is designed to keep your phone number private from other users — you should not need to share it to complete a transaction.

E.3 Minors

Locvia is intended for users 18 years and older. If you are meeting another user in person, we recommend that any accompanying minors remain under adult supervision at all times.

06

Enforcement and Contact

Violations of this Marketplace Policy will be handled in accordance with our Report & Enforcement Policy, and may result in listing removal, warnings, temporary suspension, or permanent account termination, depending on severity and recurrence.

For questions about this Marketplace Policy, contact us at hello.locvia@gmail.com.
