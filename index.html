A reputation you build

Built on trust

The problem was never distance.
It was trust.

Buying and selling online usually means dealing with someone you'll never see again. Locvia is built the other way around.

  • ✓Phone verified. Every account is verified by OTP — no anonymous throwaway profiles.
  • ✓Number stays private. Chat happens inside the app. Your phone number is never shown to another user.
  • ✓Trust Score. Every completed deal builds a reputation your neighbours can see.
  • ✓Report and block. Every listing, chat and profile can be reported or blocked, and reports are reviewed.
  • ✓No payments in-app. You meet, you check, then you decide. Nothing is paid in advance to a stranger.
