Legal

Community Guidelines

Last updated: July 2026

Locvia is a neighbourhood community, and these Community Guidelines (which include our Content Policy) exist to keep that community safe, respectful, and trustworthy. They apply to every listing, message, profile, photo, and interaction on the Service, and are incorporated by reference into our Terms & Conditions. Violation of these Guidelines may result in content removal, warnings, or account suspension or termination in accordance with our Report & Enforcement Policy.

01

Our Core Principles

Be honest — describe what you are selling or offering accurately. Be respectful — treat other users the way you would want to be treated by a neighbour. Be safe — never share sensitive personal or financial information, and always exercise caution when meeting in person. Follow the law — do not use Locvia for anything illegal under Indian law or the law of your jurisdiction.

02

Prohibited Content — Hate Speech and Discrimination

You may not post, send, or display content that promotes violence or hatred against individuals or groups based on race, ethnicity, national origin, religion, caste, disability, gender, gender identity, sexual orientation, age, or any other protected characteristic. This includes slurs, dehumanizing language, and symbols associated with hate groups.

03

Prohibited Content — Violence and Harm

You may not post content that depicts, threatens, glorifies, or incites violence against any person or animal, including graphic violence, terrorism-related content, or credible threats of harm. Content that promotes self-harm or suicide is strictly prohibited; if you or someone you know is in crisis, please contact a local emergency service or helpline immediately.

04

Prohibited Content — Sexual Content and CSAM

Sexually explicit, obscene, or pornographic content is strictly prohibited. Any content that sexualizes, exploits, or endangers minors — including child sexual abuse material (CSAM) in any form — is absolutely forbidden and will result in immediate permanent account termination, preservation of evidence, and mandatory reporting to the National Center for Missing & Exploited Children (NCMEC), the relevant Indian authorities under the POCSO Act, and any other applicable law enforcement agency, without prior notice to the user.

05

Fake Profiles, Impersonation, and Identity Theft

You may not create an account using a false identity, impersonate another individual or organization, or misrepresent your affiliation with any person or entity. Using another person's phone number, photograph, or identifying information without their consent to create or operate an account is strictly prohibited and may constitute identity theft under applicable law.

06

AI-Generated Content and Deepfakes

If you use AI-generated or digitally manipulated images, video, or text in a listing or profile, you must not present such content as an authentic photograph of the actual item, service, or person unless clearly disclosed. Deepfakes or synthetic media used to impersonate a real person, deceive users, or misrepresent goods are prohibited. As Locvia introduces AI-powered features, additional disclosure requirements will apply and will be communicated through in-app notice and updates to this Policy.

07

Harassment, Bullying, and Threats

You may not use Locvia to harass, bully, stalk, intimidate, or threaten any user, whether in listings, in-app chat, or any other feature. Repeated unwanted contact after a user has indicated they wish to stop communicating is prohibited and may result in immediate suspension.

08

Spam and Manipulation

You may not post duplicate or near-duplicate listings, use automated tools or bots to interact with the Service, artificially inflate engagement, or send unsolicited bulk messages. Listings must be genuine offers relevant to the category selected and must not be used solely to drive traffic to external platforms.

09

Political and Religious Content

Locvia is a marketplace, not a platform for political campaigning or religious proselytizing. Listings and profile content should not be used to promote political candidates, parties, or campaigns, or to solicit religious conversion. General discussion of local community matters in good faith is not prohibited by this section, but must not devolve into content otherwise prohibited under Section 2 or 7.

10

Intellectual Property and Copyright

Do not post photographs, text, logos, or other content that you do not own or do not have permission to use. Do not sell counterfeit goods bearing another party's trademark. If you believe your intellectual property rights have been infringed, please follow the copyright complaint process described in our Terms & Conditions.

11

Off-Platform Payment Solicitation

While Locvia does not currently process payments (see our Subscription Policy for future plans), users soliciting advance payment through unverified external payment links, cryptocurrency, or wire transfer — particularly before any in-person inspection of goods — should be treated with caution. Locvia may flag or restrict accounts exhibiting patterns consistent with advance-fee fraud, as described in our Marketplace Policy.

12

Reporting Violations

If you encounter content or behavior that violates these Guidelines, use the in-app Report feature available on listings, chats, and user profiles. Reports are reviewed in accordance with our Report & Enforcement Policy. Do not attempt to resolve serious violations (such as threats of violence or suspected CSAM) directly with the other user — report immediately and, where there is risk to personal safety, contact local law enforcement.

13

Enforcement

Violations of these Guidelines may result in content removal, formal warnings, temporary suspension, or permanent termination of your account, depending on the severity and frequency of the violation, at Locvia's sole discretion. Severe violations — including CSAM, credible threats of violence, or trafficking-related content — will result in immediate permanent termination without prior warning, and may be reported to law enforcement as described in Section 4 and our Privacy Policy.

14

Updates to These Guidelines

As Locvia introduces new features (including services, rentals, jobs, events, and community posts), these Guidelines will be updated to address category-specific standards. We will notify users of material changes through the app.

15

Contact Us

For questions about these Guidelines, contact us at hello.locvia@gmail.com.
